Lineup

The Experience

Four days of immersive bass music, art, and connection beneath the forest sky.

Main Stage

Powered by Subphonic Events with LED video wall, full lighting rig, and massive PA with serious bass. Matching or exceeding the 2025 setup, scaling up with ticket sales.

The Bowl

Camp's natural amphitheater — our high-energy afters stage after main shuts down. Scales from Keith's Soundboks setup to full second production based on ticket sales.

The Dome

Geodesic chill space for winding down. Relaxed vibes, comfortable seating, ambient music on a small Soundboks — a place to decompress, not rage.

640 Acres

Camp Tahquitz at 6,500ft elevation. Heated pool, Munzer Lake, historic cabins, zip line, climbing wall, and miles of forest trails.

Info & FAQ

  • Thursday: Gates open at 12 PM. Music begins at 5 PM on the Main Stage and runs until 2 AM.
  • Afters: The Bowl and The Dome remain open past sunrise every night.
  • Daytime: Programming, workshops, and activities start at 9 AM on Friday, Saturday, and Sunday.
  • Gates: Close every night at 10 PM. Reopen at 10 AM Friday through Sunday.
  • Monday: Pack out and depart by noon.

Camp Tahquitz is a 640-acre private camp in the San Bernardino Mountains, near Angelus Oaks. About 90 minutes from Los Angeles via Highway 38.

Camp Tahquitz
39000 Mill Creek Rd
Angelus Oaks, CA 92305
Google Maps Apple Maps Waze
  • From LA: Take I-10 East to Highway 38 (near Redlands). Follow 38 through the mountains to Mill Creek Rd.
  • Gates: Open Thursday 12:00 PM. Early arrival Wednesday with add-on.
  • Check-in: Ticket + ID (18+). Wristbands at the gate.
  • Re-entry: Allowed. Come and go as needed.
  • Late arrivals: No nightly cut-off.
  • Parking: General areas available. Car camping add-on for campsite parking.

Camping Options

  • GA Parking + Tent Camping — Included with every wristband
  • Car Camping — $30 add-on. Park your vehicle at your campsite.
  • RV Camping — $100 add-on. Dry camping only (no power, water, or sewer hookups). Bring your own RV. Limited to 5 spots.
  • Heated Cabins — $1,000 each. 6 historic cabins available, sleeps 9 per cabin.

Campground Info

  • Munzer Lake available for swimming and boating
  • Leave No Trace: Pack it in, pack it out
What is the age requirement?

18+ only. Valid government-issued ID required at check-in.

What items are prohibited?

Weapons, illegal items, and glass containers are strictly forbidden. Wood fires are not allowed — propane stoves and grills only.

Is there harm reduction on site?

Yes. Trained medical volunteers, naloxone available, and shaded rest areas. Your safety is our priority.

Can I bring alcohol?

Yes, BYO. No alcohol sales on site. Please drink responsibly.

Are meal plans available?

Yes! Full Festival ($100/8 meals), Weekend ($50/4 meals), or Single Day ($30/2 meals). Purchase during checkout.

What is the refund policy?

All sales are final. If the event is cancelled, full refunds will be issued.

Is there cell service?

Limited. Some carriers work better than others at 6,500ft elevation. Download offline content before arriving.
