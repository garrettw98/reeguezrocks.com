Financial Transparency

Every dollar explained. We operate on a community-funded model where production scales fluidly and margins build the experience.

The Scaling Model

We prioritize safety and sound above all else. To achieve this, **Fixed Body Costs** are allocated from every ticket first. As more people join, the Operations budget (Medical/Security) increases to maintain professional standards.

Per-Guest "Body" Costs
Venue Access Fee $10.00 / day
Stage Production (The Bowl + Dome) $37.00 flat
Fluid Ops (Medical, Safety, Waste) $10.00 - $30.00
Processing & Referral Fees ~$8.00 - $11.00

Sustainable Margins

After the "Body Costs" are covered, the remaining margin goes directly into the Crowdfunding Tiers to pay for headliners, art, and lasers.

Community Fund

Beyond the tiers, we maintain a **10% Community Fund**. This pool is used post-event to reward artists who performed for the margin-pool and volunteers who went above and beyond to keep the forest clean.
