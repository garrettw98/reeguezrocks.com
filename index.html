A Sonic Journey in Nature's Embrace

Reeguez Rocks 2024 unites an incredible community of music lovers in a breathtaking natural setting for our fourth annual gathering.

  • Connect with a diverse, welcoming crowd of passionate electronic music enthusiasts
  • Experience a wide-ranging lineup showcasing both emerging talents and established underground artists
  • Immerse yourself in the stunning beauty of rugged landscapes and star-filled desert skies

Our carefully curated roster of artists spans the electronic spectrum, from deep, introspective soundscapes to bass-heavy bangers. Each set offers a unique journey, enhanced by the natural surroundings and open-air atmosphere.

The untamed wilderness provides a one-of-a-kind backdrop for this intimate festival. Move to the rhythms among ancient rock formations, with majestic mountain ranges painting an awe-inspiring horizon.

Join us for three days of music, connection, and natural wonder. Reeguez Rocks 2024: Where passionate people, talented artists, and the raw beauty of nature converge to create an unforgettable experience.
