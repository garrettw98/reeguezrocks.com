Lineup

The Experience

Four days of immersive bass music, art, and connection beneath the forest sky.

Main Stage

Powered by Subphonic Events with LED video wall, programmable lighting, and high-quality audio. Scales from $3k to $15k based on tiers unlocked.

Campfire Bowl

Natural amphitheater with excellent acoustics for late-night afters. Keith's Soundboks system powers the party past sunrise.

The Dome

Geodesic dome chill lounge for ambient and downtempo vibes. A space for connection, not a competing stage. ~30 person capacity.

640 Acres

Camp Tahquitz at 6,500ft elevation. Heated pool, Munzer Lake, historic cabins, zip line, climbing wall, and miles of forest trails.

Event Info

  • Thursday: Gates open 12:00 PM — Music begins 5:00 PM
  • Nights: Main Stage runs until 2:00 AM
  • Afters: Campfire Bowl continues past sunrise
  • Daytime: Yoga, workshops, swimming, activities
  • The Dome: Ambient chill space open all weekend

Essentials

  • Water: 1 gallon/person/day minimum
  • Sun protection: hat, sunglasses, SPF 30+
  • Warm layers: jacket, beanie, gloves (nights 30-45°F)
  • Sturdy closed-toe shoes for forest terrain
  • Headlamp + extra batteries
  • Power banks for phones

Camping

  • Tent rated for cooler temps
  • Stakes (it gets windy)
  • Sleeping bag + extra blankets
  • Food for 4+ days (or buy meal plan)
  • Propane stove (no wood fires)
  • Trash bags (pack it out)

Nice to Have

  • Hearing protection for the rail
  • Swimwear for pool and lake
  • Camp chairs
  • First aid kit
  • Full tank of gas + spare tire

Camp Tahquitz is private property surrounded by San Bernardino National Forest, approximately 90 minutes from Los Angeles. Detailed directions emailed to ticket holders.

  • Gates: Open Thursday 12:00 PM. Early arrival Wednesday with add-on.
  • Check-in: Ticket + ID (18+). Wristbands at the gate.
  • Re-entry: Allowed. Come and go as needed.
  • Late arrivals: No nightly cut-off.
  • Parking: General areas available. Car camping add-on for campsite parking.
  • Tent camping included with all GA tickets
  • Car camping available as $30 add-on
  • 6 historic heated cabins available ($1,000 each, sleeps 9)
  • Heated pool and Munzer Lake for swimming/boating
  • Propane only — no wood fires allowed
  • Bear boxes throughout venue — store all food properly
  • Leave No Trace: Pack it in, pack it out

FAQ

What is the age requirement?

18+ only. Valid government-issued ID required at check-in.

What items are prohibited?

Weapons, illegal items, and glass containers are strictly forbidden. Wood fires are not allowed — propane stoves and grills only.

Is there harm reduction on site?

Yes. Trained medical volunteers, naloxone available, and shaded rest areas. Your safety is our priority.

Can I bring alcohol?

Yes, BYO. No alcohol sales on site. Please drink responsibly.

Are meal plans available?

Yes! Full Festival ($100/8 meals), Weekend ($50/4 meals), or Single Day ($30/2 meals). Purchase during checkout.

What is the refund policy?

All sales are final. If the event is cancelled, full refunds will be issued.

Is there cell service?

Limited. Some carriers work better than others at 6,500ft elevation. Download offline content before arriving.
