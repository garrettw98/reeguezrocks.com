3 Days of Impeccable Underground Vibes

Welcome to Reeguez Rocks 2024 - where amazing artists, incredible scenery, and unforgettable experiences converge in the breathtaking Alabama Hills!

What began as a few friends throwing down among the rocks has blossomed into something truly magical. Our fourth annual gathering offers:

  • A diverse musical journey spanning dubstep, experimental bass, drum and bass, riddim, house, techno, and trance
  • An intimate, community-driven atmosphere that values the raw, unfiltered essence of electronic music culture
  • A unique blend of stunning natural backdrops and cutting-edge sounds
  • A platform for various forms of artistic expression, creating an immersive experience

Reeguez Rocks isn't just an event; it's a gathering of like-minded individuals who appreciate underground electronic music in its purest form. From the carefully curated lineup to the awe-inspiring scenery, every aspect is designed to resonate with true music enthusiasts and adventure seekers alike.

How to Join the Experience

Reeguez Rocks is an exclusive, community-focused event. Here's what you need to know:

  • If you've found this website, you're already part of our extended family - welcome!
  • To cover event costs, we operate on a donation-based model
  • Your contribution goes directly towards enhancing the experience: audio/visual rentals, amenities, communal supplies, and creating the perfect vibe
  • In return for your support, you'll be added to our mailing list for exclusive event information and updates

We're not here to make a profit - our goal is to create the best possible microfestival for our Reeguez Rocks family. Your support helps us maintain the intimate, high-quality experience that makes this event special.

Join us in crafting an unforgettable journey of music, nature, and community!
