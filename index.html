Pack List & What to Expect

  • Elevation: Camp Tahquitz is at 6,500ft. Expect cooler temperatures than the desert — highs 60s–70s°F, nights 30–45°F.
  • Water: Bring at least 1 gallon per person per day for drinking/cooking. Potable water may be available on site.
  • Sun Protection: Wide‑brim hat, sunglasses, sunscreen, lip balm — high altitude means stronger UV.
  • Warm Layers: Insulated jacket, beanie, gloves — mountain nights get cold.
  • Footwear: Sturdy closed‑toe shoes/boots for forest terrain.
  • Lighting & Power: Headlamp + extra batteries; power banks for phones.
  • Hearing Protection: It gets loud on the rail, protect yourself!
  • Camp: Stakes for tent; solid tent rated for cooler temps; extra blankets or sleeping bag.
  • Food: Enough for 4+ days, or purchase a meal plan.
  • Fire Safety: No wood fires allowed — propane stoves and grills only. Bear country protocols required.
  • Bears: Use bear boxes provided throughout the venue. Never leave food in tents.
  • Trash: Pack it in, pack it out. Leave No Trace principles — bring extra trash bags.
  • Vehicle Prep: Full tank of gas, spare tire + jack. Mountain roads to venue.
  • First Aid: Personal meds, small kit; medical volunteers on site.
  • Swimwear: Heated pool and Munzer Lake available for swimming!

Camping & Stages

Main Stage: Powered by Subphonic Events with a full PA system, LED video wall, and programmable lighting. Budget scales with crowdfunding tiers ($3k–$12k). Main Stage programming runs until 2:00 AM each night.

Campfire Bowl: Natural amphitheater with excellent acoustics for late-night afters. Keith's Soundboks system powers the party past sunrise.

The Dome: Keith's geodesic dome structure — ambient/downtempo vibes only. A chill space for connection, not a competing stage. ~30 person capacity.

  • Tent camping included with all GA tickets. Car camping available as add-on ($30).
  • 6 historic heated cabins available ($1,000 each, sleeps 9).
  • Heated swimming pool and Munzer Lake for swimming and boating.
  • Propane stoves/grills only — no wood fires allowed.
  • Bear boxes throughout the venue — store all food properly.
  • Leave No Trace: Pack it in, pack it out — do not leave trash.
