- [ ] Build email list from past attendees
- [ ] Prepare 2 weeks of social content
- [ ] Secure 1-2 conditional artist LOIs
- [ ] Set up affiliate tracking system

### Launch Week (Feb 1-7)
- **Day 1:** Launch with video + conditional artist announcement
- **Day 2-3:** Email blast to past attendees/community
- **Day 4-5:** Instagram/TikTok push + Discord engagement

- **Goal:** Start Phase 3 (Tier 6 - $26,500)
- **Focus:** Experience additions
- **Message:** "Look what early backers unlocked"

### Month 4: Growth Push (May)
- **Goal:** Complete Phase 3 (Tier 9 - $42,000)
- **Focus:** Second + third headliner
- **Message:** "Festival is really coming together"

### Month 5: Expansion (June)
- **Goal:** Complete Phase 4 (Tier 12 - $68,000)

### Month 6: Final Push (July)

### Month 7: Wrap Up (August-September)
