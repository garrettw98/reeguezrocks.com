## 1. General Event Information

*   **Capacity:** **2,000 – 2,500 People** (Hard cap TBC by venue)

## 2. Core Philosophy & Funding
*   **Model:** 100% Community-Funded (Crowdfunding)
*   **Funding Goal (Minimum):** $6,500 (Tier 1). **Target Date:** June 1, 2026.
*   **Funding Goal (Stretch):** $95,000 (Tier 15) for full "Marquee" experience.
*   **Refund Policy:** 100% Refund if Tier 1 is not reached. Funds held in Stripe; manual refund issuance.

## 3. Venue & Infrastructure
### Stages
*   **The Bowl:** Amphitheater. Primary stage for intimate crowds / Secondary stage for late-night.
*   **The Meadow:** Large open field. Primary stage for larger crowds (Phase 3+).
*   **The Dome:** 28ft Geodesic Dome. Chill/Downtempo sanctuary.

### Camping & Accommodation
*   **GA Camping:** Walk-in (park in lot, walk to forest). Included in ticket.
*   **Car Camping:** Park at campsite. Limited availability ($30 add-on).
*   **RV Camping:** Dry camping only. Limited (<10 spots) ($100 add-on).
*   **Cabins:** Historic heated cabins (Sleeps 9). 6 available ($1,000).

### Facilities & Logistics
*   **Parking:** Ample on-site capacity (Venue handles thousands of scouts).
*   **Sanitation:** Venue infrastructure sufficient for base tiers. Contingency: Rental Porta-potties/Showers if >1,500 attendees.
*   **Trash:** Managed by Venue (Summer Camp infrastructure).
*   **Medical:** Venue **Health Lodge** (stocked) is primary. Ambulance standby contingent on County requirements/size (2,000+).

### Rules & Policies
*   **Power:** **NO FUEL GENERATORS.** Solar/Battery only for sound camps.
*   **Fire:** No wood fires. Propane stoves only. Venue designed with fire lanes/access.
*   **Prohibited:** Glass, weapons, illegal substances.

### Permits & Compliance
*   **Status:** Not yet in motion. Owner to begin process next.
*   **Requirements:** Fire Permit ($150), County Permit ($500).
