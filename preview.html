Getting to Space Ranch

The Mojave is calling. We’ll send detailed driving directions to ticket holders — here’s what to know before you roll out.

Final pin: Space Ranch — Mojave Desert, CA (between Boron and Ridgecrest). Coordinates: 35°13'11.6"N 117°27'01.8"W.

  • Allow 3 hours from LA, 4.5 from Sacramento.
  • Pack 1–1.5 gallons of water per person per day.
  • Bring shade, dust protection, and warm nighttime layers.
