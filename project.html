Revolutionizing Festival Participation

Welcome to our Community-Driven Festival Experience, where music lovers become part of the festival's success. We're revolutionizing the festival experience by offering early bird memberships with revenue sharing opportunities.

Early Bird Membership Process

  1. Early Bird Ticket Release: We announce a limited number of early bird tickets for our curated festival experience.
  2. Community Participation: Members purchase early bird tickets, securing their attendance and a stake in the festival's success.
  3. Milestone-Based Execution: As ticket sales thresholds are met, we collaboratively secure venues, artists, and enhance the event experience.
  4. Festival Realization: Together, we bring the community-backed festival to life, with early bird members enjoying VIP status.
  5. Revenue Sharing: Post-festival, profits are calculated and 50% is distributed evenly among early bird members, on top of the unforgettable experience.

By participating as an early bird member, you're not just buying a ticket – you're becoming part of a unique festival experience you help create. Enjoy potential financial returns, exclusive perks, and a stronger community connection. Join us in shaping the future of live events while potentially benefiting from the festival's success.

Frequently Asked Questions

What happens if the event doesn't make a profit?

In the unlikely event that we don't make a profit, early bird members will receive a proportional share of the remaining funds after event costs are covered.

How do I receive my share of the profits?

Profits will be distributed electronically to the payment method you used for your early bird ticket purchase within 30 days after the event.
